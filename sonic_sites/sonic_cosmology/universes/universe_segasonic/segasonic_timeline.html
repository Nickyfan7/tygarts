Begins







Dr. Eggman Attacks South Island!

Dr. Eggman begins capturing its animals and placing them inside of Robots called "Badniks" and Egg Prisons in search of the legendary Chaos Emeralds so he may conquer the world. Sonic is on a run through Green Hill when he witnesses a Motobug attacking Flickies, and defeats it.

The Flicky explains Dr. Eggman's ambitions to Sonic who journeys across South Island collects the Chaos Emeralds and takes to Scrap Brain where Dr. Eggman is defeated, and Sonic uses the Chaos Emeralds to restore peace to South Island where he's given a vision of a future adventure.

Little Planet Appears, Amy Rose Kidnapped!

Little Planet is set to appear over Never Lake every one month, a year. Dr. Eggman discovers the Time Stones, and seeks to claim their power for himself establishing a mountain base, building Robot Generators and Metal Sonic.

Amy Rose, a young girl with and interest in Tarrot Cards, preforms a reading at Never Lake that reveals she's to have a chance encounter on Little Planet as it descends. Dr. Eggman chains Little Planet to Earth as both Amy and him make their way to the planet.

Sonic arrives at Never Lake only to find Little Planet overtaken by Dr. Eggman, climbing the chain where he ends up in Palmtree Panic undoing Eggman's meddling with time, meeting Amy along the way who follows him to Collision Chaos.

Once there they find themselves at an impasse when Amy is kidnapped by Metal Sonic! Sonic continues his journey destroying all the Robot Generators, and collecting all 7 Time Stones before defeating Metal Sonic and rescuing Amy.

Sonic once again defeats Dr. Eggman in Metallic Madness causing it to self-destruct, escaping alongside Amy back to Earth where he destroys the chain holding Little Planet to Earth - Little Planet returns back into space. Sonic takes his biplane off in search of new adventures.

Notes/Contradictions

  • Sonic the Hedgehog 4: According to SEGA there's canonically only one Metal Sonic, Sonic 4 shows he remained on Little Planet until Episode Metal. This contradicts Sonic Triple Trouble, Knuckles Chaotix, Sonic the Fighters and Sonic Mania Adventures.




An Unbreakable Bond, and Fallen Death Eggs

Sonic discovers the Island he saw in his vision back during his first adventure - Westside Island, once said to house an ancient civilization that used a Chaos Emerald to achieve prosperity, quickly falling to their own greed an hubris when they abused its power, with it being sealed away in the Island's depths.

Sometime after landing on the Island, Sonic saw a young fox boy named Miles "Tails" Prower being bullied and quickly subdued the bullies. Tails inspired by such a sight wanted to be more like Sonic and decided to follow him discovering he could use his Tails to fly as a means of keeping up with him.

Tails eventually discovers Sonic's Biplane, having an interest in technology he inspects it mere moments before the Island is set a flame by attacking Badniks. Dr. Eggman has detected the Chaos Emeralds and seeks to collect them all to power his Death Egg.

Sonic and Tails set off on a journey across Westside Island's Chemical Plants, Oil Refineries, and Factories collecting all 7 Chaos Emeralds as Sonic makes his way to the Death Egg where he once again defeats Dr. Eggman, escaping from the exploding vessel out a nearby airlock where Super Sonic flies by his buddy.

Meanwhile, the Death Egg crash-lands on the mysterious Angel Island which slowly begins sinking into the sea below.

After Sonic 2's Adventure...

The Death Egg falls and crash-lands on Angel Island, causing immense destruction as Angel Island crashes into the ocean with enough force to generate a tsunami. Dr. Eggman exits the Death Egg robot in Hidden Palace where he discovers the Master Emerald only to meet it's guardian - a reclusive echidna named Knuckles.

Knuckles prepares to attack, only for Eggman to plead with him twisting the truth explaining that he was simply trying to conduct research only to be attacked by Sonic and Tails. This convinces Knuckles who sets off in preparation for Sonic and Tails's arrival setting traps all over Angel Island.

Meanwhile Dr. Eggman builds Launch Base preparing to get his Death Egg back online, conspiring to use the Master Emerald to repair it. Elsewhere Tails detects a large Chaos Emerald Energy signature and believing it's connected to the tsunami finds Sonic on the beach, where he agrees to investigate.




Dr. Eggman's Conspiracy

Sonic and Tails arrive on Angel Island and are ambushed by Knuckles who steals the Chaos Emeralds. Sonic and Tails travel across Angel Island, and re-collect them, ending up in Launch Base.

Sonic and Tails, defeat Dr. Eggman and thwart the Death Egg's Re-Launch, flying to the nearby Mushroom Hill as the Death Egg Crash-lands back on Angel Island. Sonic and Tails discover Knuckles hiding a secret entrance.

In Hidden Palace, Sonic and Tails bear witness to the summoning of the Super Emeralds and collect all 7. Eventually ending up back at Hidden Palace where they face Knuckles and defeat him as Dr. Eggman electrocutes Knuckles and steals the Master Emerald.

Knuckles leads them to Sky Sanctuary, activating a bridge as the Death Egg Launches and deploys an army of Egg-robos. Sonic and Tails make a mad-dash to reach the Death Egg beating Mecha Sonic Mk.II as they board the Death Egg.

Sonic and Tails blast their way through Red Eye and Death Ball taking the Fight to Dr. Eggman who escapes into a nearby Asteroid Belt. Sonic transforms into Hyper Sonic for the first, and only time to give chase,

Dr. Eggman is defeated and Sonic and Tails returns the Master Emerald to Angel Island as Dr. Eggman is left stranded at sea. The Super Emeralds disappear into an unknown dimension leaving only stone relics as a reminder of their existence.

Mecha Sonic Locks-On!!

Unbeknownst to the Trio, back in Sky Sanctuary Mecha Sonic Mk.II survied and reactivates an Eggrobo planning to steal and deliver the Master Emerald to Dr.Eggman. Meanwhile Knuckles rests in Mushroom Hill when he's bombed by Eggrobo who leads him away from Hidden Palace.

Whilst Knuckles is distracted Mecha Sonic steals the Master Emerald, as Eggrobo reports back. Knuckles winds up back in Angel Island (Zone), realizing he's been duped he heads off on an adventure to reclaim the Master Emerald.

Knuckles makes his way to Sky Sanctuary where he faces off against Mecha Sonic, who despite his Super Form is defeated. Thanks to the help of Sonic in the Tornado, Knuckles returns the Master Emerald to Angel Island.

The badly-damaged Mecha Sonic falls into the ocean being left adrift for quite some time until eventually washing up on Scrapnik Island.






Dr. Eggman Attacks South Island...Again!

Dr. Eggman is back to his old tricks, as he once again attacks South Island in search of the Chaos Emeralds and imprisons the Island's wildlife in robots. Sonic ventures across new areas of South Island collecting the Chaos Emeralds and defeats him as he escapes in Sky Base using the Chaos Emeralds to restore peace to South Island.




Classic Sonic ripped from the Past!

Sonic runs through Green Hill when he's ripped from his own point in time by the Time Eater and ends up in White Space. Where he eventually meets up with Tails in front of Green Hill before arriving aboard the Death Egg, where he faces Dr. Eggman's Death Egg Robot, before he's kidnapped by the Time Eater.

After defeat, Sonic returns to White Space where it's revealed that they've traveled through time, meaning the reflection Sonic saw in the Death Egg was his future self. Classic and Modern Sonic team up to restore space-time collecting the Chaos Emeralds along the way.

Soon they find themselves facing off against the Time Eater piloted by Modern and Classic Eggman who explain how they found it and teamed up before incapacitating them. Modern and Classic Sonic transform into Super Sonic and defeat the Time Eater returning to Modern Sonic's Time where they enjoy their birthday party.

After the party ends, Classic Sonic and Tails return to their own time. Classic Eggman is left trapped in White Space with Modern Eggman and through as of now unknown means he manages to return to his own point in time.

Notes/Contradictions:

  • Shadow Generations & Plot Hole: It's never canonically explained how Modern and Classic Eggman escaped White Space. Though in Shadow Generations, Shadow helps Orbot and Cubot build a rocket that turns out to be a clock - it can be headcannoned that Dr. Eggman would modify it into a functional rocket.



Foxnapped! Tails is captured!

Dr. Eggman returns from White Space and has collected the Chaos Emeralds in secret, building 6 Master Robots to guard them before kidnapping the Animals of South Island and imprisoning them in his robots once again. Unfortunately, among them was Tails, who Dr. Eggman imprisons in Crystal Egg.

Sonic soon arrives at Tails's Workshop only to find a note by Tails warning him of Dr. Eggman's plans, his imprisonment, and how Crystal Egg can only be entered with the Chaos Emeralds. Sonic begins his journey across South Island defeating Dr. Eggman's Master Robots and collecting the Chaos Emeralds.

Sonic recovers the last emerald after defeating Mecha Sonic Mk.0 in the Scrambled Egg as Dr. Eggman to flees into Crystal Egg with Sonic in pursuit making it through Crystal Egg Sonic defeats Dr. Eggman and rescues Tails.

South Island Sinks!

Dr. Eggman obtains the Red Chaos Emerald disrupting their connection to South Island in the process, causing it to sink into the sea. Sonic and Tails hear rumors that Dr. Eggman is searching for the Chaos Emeralds and return to South Island only to find the rumors were true.

Sonic and Tails soon track down and give chase to Dr. Eggman who escapes with the Red Chaos Emerald, making their way to Turqouise Hill where they journey across South Island collecting all the Chaos Emeralds and defeating Dr. Eggman. Sonic uses the Chaos Emeralds to restore South Island.
