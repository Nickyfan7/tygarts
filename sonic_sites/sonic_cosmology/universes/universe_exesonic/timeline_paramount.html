The Master Emerald War (Mobius, ???? A.D.)

  • The Big Bang explodes creating the multiverse within it residing various universes this being one and in this universe exists various planets, such as Mobius, Earth, and the Mushroom Planet alongside these planets creation would be the Chaos Emeralds - 7 Gems of Unquantifiable Power.
  • Through unknown means the Chaos Emeralds would come to rest on Planet Mobius where they'd be discovered by the Ancient Echidna Tribe who take note of their great power and forge them into the Master Emerald - the most powerful weapon in the universe using it as a tool to seize other lands.
  • A war breaks out between the Owl Clan and Echidna Tribe, during it a group from the Owl Clan steals the Master Emerald and hides it away on Earth believing nobody should weild such power.

Project Shadow (Earth, 1974 A.D.)

  • A meteorite crash-lands in Oklahoma containing the Ultimate Lifeform: Shadow the Hedgehog. The U.S. Military seizes the Meteorite with Shadow still inside and assign Commander Walters and Gerald Robotnik to the classified "Project Shadow" experiement.
  • Commander Walters and Gerald believing Shadow's Chaos Energy would usher in a new era of humanity seeks to use it for Military and Civilian purposes, despite this the U.S. Government fears the dangers of Shadow's Chaos Energy falling into the wrong hands.
  • As such the U.S. Government calls on the Guardian Units of Nations (aka G.U.N) to shut down the project and seize all assets - including Shadow himself. Gerald would find out about this and attempt to escape with Shadow and his granddaughter Maria, with Walters, GUN Agents, and U.S. Special Forces following them to the exit.
  • The Military prepares to open fire only for Commander Walters to intervine, leading a tank of Chaos Energy to be shot and induce an explosion that takes the life of Maria Robotnik, Commander Walters had Gerald Arrested.
  • Commander Walters, seeing Shadow as "Too Dangerous to Wander, Too Valuable to Kill.", had him placed into stasis before working with other government officials to cover up all traces of the project. Despite these efforts some files remained connecting Robotnik to the Project.

The Whipple Shabbat Dinner (Earth, 1980 A.D.)

  • In 1980, Wendy Whipple holds a Shabbat Dinner at the Whipple Household - things quicky devolve into chaos thanks to Wade and Wanda Whipple much to Wendy's Exasperation as the Shabbat ends in Disaster. This becomes a regular occurance between them every Shabat much to Wanda's Frustration.

The Flames of Disaster, and Earth Traveler
(Galaxy-Wide, Earth, 2010/2013 A.D.)

  • In 2013, The Echidna Tribe Locates the last member of the Owl Clan - Longclaw the Giant Owl, seeking the location of the Master Emerald the Chaos Energy of Her Apprentice, they prepare to invade. A young Knuckles trained by his father wishes to join but is told by his Father to stay behind.
  • The Invasion Begins and forces Longclaw to equip Sonic with a bag of Rings before sending him to Earth - her last request being to remain in hiding and should he be found escape to the Mushroom Planet, before taking the fight to the Echidnas managing to fend them off before succumbing to her injures.
  • Knuckles learns of Sonic's identity and, believing him to be the key to the Master Emerald, searches the cosmos to find him. Along the way Knuckles sought out the Fire-Demon, Iblis - for the secrets of it's power: The Flames of Disaster. He defeats Iblis granting him access to the Flames of Disaster.

Notes:

  • It's never really made clear when Sonic arrived on Earth but the movies/external media do provide context clues that give us some ideas.
    • In Sonic the Hedgehog, after the scene where Sonic arrives on Earth we're told 10 years have passed. This is contradicted by Knuckles (2024) in which Sonic states he's "Lived in a cave for 7 Years."
  • How Knuckles gained the Flames of Disaster is explained in Knuckles (2024), but the series has various contradictions with the films. Sonic the Hedgehog 3 confirms he did gain access to the power however, making the series soft-canon.
    • An example of this can be seen in the episode The Flames of Disaster where during the rock-opera, it's explained that after the invasion Longclaw destroyed the Echidna Village and killed Knuckles Father in front of him.
    • The above contradicts Sonic the Hedgehog 2 where we see what happened moments prior to the invasion - Knuckles' Father tells him to stay behind before heading off, Knuckles recalls this being the last time he saw his father - implying he was not a witness to his death.

Notes

  • Note: It seems likely that Knuckles (2024 is soft-canon. This is because in Sonic the Hedgehog 3 - Knuckles uses the Flames of Disaster, a power introduced in this series yet the series also carries some inconsistencies with the movies.
    • The Warrior: Sonic visits Knuckles in the attic where he states he's lived in a cave for 7 years however in Sonic the Hedgehog it's stated he's been on Earth for at least 10 Years.
    • The Flames of Disaster: In Sonic the Hedgehog 2 we see a scene depicting the last time Knuckles saw his father before leaving to find Longclaw. In this episode - it's said Longclaw wiped out the Echidna Tribe and came to destroy the Echidna Village.
      • All this backstory is implied to have occured before Sonic fled to Earth - making it impossible for Longclaw to have attacked the village as Sonic the Hedgehog implies her death.
    • What Happens in Reno, Stays in Reno: Agent Wiloughby is defeated after Knuckles deflects 2 rings shot at him which opens them in an Infinite Loop. In Sonic the Hedgehog 2, Sonic explains that Rings work when the user thinks of a location and throws it, thus opening another ring in the desired location.

      This renders infinite ring loop seen in this episode impossible, as it would instead cause four rings to manifest in the room at once, 2 rings being the destination of the other 2 rings.
