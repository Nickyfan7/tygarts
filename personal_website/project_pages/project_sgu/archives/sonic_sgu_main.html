What is this?

Sonic the Hedgehog: Genesis Unified is an alernate take on the Sonic the Hedgehog Series Canon which aims to do what Servive Games (SEGA) struggles to - bring all of Sonic the Hedgehog's 30+ Years of Media into one cohesive universe/canon. This will be done by rewriting or recontextualizing certain events from the Sonic Games, Comics, TV Shows, Movies, and more. There are also plans to add some fangame stories into the mix as well under the pretense that they cannot contradict established canon or may only be included if it may be rewritten to work in-canon.

What is Canon?

Before revealing what's canon some things of note: Some of the Media in the Sonic Series contains adaptations of the Sonic the Hedgehog Games - for this reason details from those adaptations will likely be merged into the original game as to keep things concise and make a more "complete" version of that game lore-wise. This means only the original stuff from Archie, Fleetway, etc will be shown in this list - with some exceptions namely anything made/written by Ken Penders.
