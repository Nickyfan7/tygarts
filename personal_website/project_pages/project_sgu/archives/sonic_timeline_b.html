The Beginning of the Multiverse/Origin of the Chaos Emeralds (??? B.S.)

  • The Big Bang explodes creating the SEGASonic Universe within it residing various planets, beings, and the Chaos Emeralds, this universe would be the Core of the Sonic the Hedgehog Multiverse.
    • Amisdt the planets created were Earth, Little Planet, The Ancients Homeworld, and The Lost Hex.
    • Amisdt the beings created are The End, Light Gaia, and Dark Gaia. Light and Dark Gaia being created in a state of slumber waking up every 1000 years for the Time of Awakening. The End goes on to destroy planet across the galaxy taking many civilizations with them.
      • Time of Awakening: An event that occurs every 1000 years, during which Dark Gaia awakens and destroys the Earth - Light Gaia would lull Dark Gaia back into rest before restoring the planet.
  • The Anceints would soon become aware of The End and build spaceships powered by Chaos Engines - engines that can tap into the Chaos Emerald's power to escape their planet mere moments before it's destroyed.
    • The Ancients notice the Chaos Emeralds reacting to something unbeknownst to them that it's the Master Emerald back on Earth. They follow the reaction of the Emeralds leading them to the Starfall Islands.
    • Sometime after their arrival on the Starfall Islands, they feared the possible return of The End as such created Cyber Space and the Titans. Cyber Space capable of archiving neural networks.
    • The End arrives and begins attacking the Ancients, leading them to mobilize the Titans despite their great power they stand no chance against The End leading Crane - Pilot of the Titan Supreme to bind The End to his Titan resulting in his death.
    • The other Titan Pilots seal The End in Cyber Space, back on Earth the remaining Ancients flee to the Mystic Ruins taking the Master Emerald with them. Years pass as the Ancients DNA degrades leading to the creation of new creatures - The Koco and The Chao.
  • Elsewhere on Earth a Jungle would grown on an unknown Island - this Island would be the Meta Junglira.
