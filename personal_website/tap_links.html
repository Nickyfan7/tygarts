Revival Servers

Revival Servers for Old Game Consoles and their Games/Apps that emulate their online connections and miscellaneous services (such as Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection, Nintendo Network, and WiiConnect24) to bring your console back online. Some also bring back defunct online services such as with Flipnote Studio's Flipnote Hatena (Sudomemo) and Flipnote Gallery: World (Kaeru Gallery)

Internet Arcade & Console Living Room

The Internet Arcade is a section of the Internet Archive that hosts thousands of Arcade Games complete with Controller Support all playable from inside your web browser of choice! The Console Living room is the same but for home consoles from Generation 1 - 5 making games from Atari to Playstation 1 playable with some ROM Hacks also being availiable so if your find yourself bored on the internet, instead of doomscrolling why not revisit some old classics?

Chats

When it comes to chat it's becoming clear that Discord is becoming the king of chats - and quickly which isn't always a good thing, and while an alternative popped up in Guilded it was eventually purchased by Roblox who promised that Guilded will remain the same - this didn't convince the users who had been burned one too many times however. As such people seem to sadly be stuck in Discords Ecosystem.

Internet Relay Chats (IRC) and Matrix are two real-time chat protocols IRC being the oldest of them and while it may seem stuck in the past it's actually made great strides since it's early days, regardless you will need to get a client for both. Revolt, Nervitia, and Nermity are more clear-cut Discord Alternatives with Revolt being among the biggest.
