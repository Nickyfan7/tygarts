About

I'm Tyranger, a 25 year old graphic designer, and pixel artist that has wanted to study Video Game Design which lead me to study design in Interactive Digital Arts, where I got my Adobe Photoshop Certification. I've wanted to become a game designer since the thought struck me when playing Nintendo's Mario Party DS and being inspired by SEGA's Sonic the Hedgehog Series, which soon led me to be given the idea of what would become Skye's Origin - a metroidvania game set on the fictional Planet Treah with a focus on lore and exploration. As such I have experience with HTML, CSS, Game Programming, and Design of Various Kinds though not the best at all of it I alway believe there's room to improve over time!
