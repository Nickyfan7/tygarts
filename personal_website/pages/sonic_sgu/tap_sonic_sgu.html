What is this?

Way back on January 14th, 2021 - SEGA posted a tweet that asserted that in regards to Sonic the Hedgehog - "Everything is Canon" basically implying all of the media in Sonic is canon. While this was initially said as a joke it appears as though SEGA wants to make this a reality but struggles to make it so due to things like the Sonic Mandates, the SEGA Regional Depratment rivalry, and the mountain of contradictory information various pieces of Sonic Media such as the TV Shows and Comics - this has led some controversy to spark around the idea of everything being canon. The aim of Sonic: Genesis Unified is to make an alternate universe where everything is canonical to said universe.

Sonic the Hedgehog: Genesis Unified is an alernate take on the Sonic the Hedgehog Series Canon which aims to make everything in the Sonic the Hedgehog series canon to one cohesive universe/canon with a major exception - any characters created by Ken Penders will not be incorporated. This will be done by rewriting, recontextualizing, or even merging some adventures from the Sonic Games, Comics, TV Shows, Movies, and more. However as this is a big project it cannot be hosted on this website and will have it's own website.

What is Canon?

What's canon depends on the universe you visit as Sonic Genesis Unified encompases a multiverse with many universes for instance there's a universe called "Alterzon" which is a universe that carries the same events but incorporates fan-made adventures (e.g. Sonic Before the Sequel, Sonic and the Fallen Star, and more).
