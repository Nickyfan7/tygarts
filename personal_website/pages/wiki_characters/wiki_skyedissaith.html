Description

Skye Eugine Dissaith is a Young Tiger Boy, who's in-tune with his own emotions alongside the emotions of others, being very introspective and often deep in thought. He feels things very deeply, both good and bad and is a natural healer. Skye carries a sense of empathy with him, able to understand how others feel, and relate to them based on similar experiences of his own, seeking to be genuinely and authentically himself.

Skye may appear meek and shy but has a fire inside, often rushing to defend the underdog where he can. He carries a passion for his hobbies and things that speak to him. Despite this he can react emotionally at times, in extreme circumstances this can lead him to lash-out even if not his intention. He may also focus on his self-image a bit too much leeding to feelings of self-doubt, insecurity, and loss of control.

All that said, Skye wants to be a good person as he has in his own words "seen the darkness in the world" - having been abused, "taken advantage of", witnessing death, and his loved ones also being "taken advantage of" in early childhood serves to fuel his empathy and fight for better days - even if he won't live to see those days.

Backstory

Skye's Story begins at just 5 years old, having been taken from his Mother and Father - Keynn and Jakii Dissaith by the CPS where he'd be adopted by Brenda Agape. Whilst under her care Skye would be beaten, burned,gaslit, and manipulated. once having his head beaten against the bathtubwall until he bled before having it dunked underwater, held, and locked in the bathroom with a bucket of poison chemicals.

In those days Skye was often sent to bed without being fed, and dressed poorly. As a result of this Skye didn't have many friends, as the kids at school would bully him relentlessly, the teachers often yelling at him due to his lack of focus. This left Skye feeling depresssed and doubtful he'd ever reunite with his birth parents, luckily for him an old colleage of Brenda's would show up bringing her child with her.

The young fennec would introduce herself to him as "Andy" as the two would go upstairs and play with the young girl treating Skye with geunine kindness, and sense of caring by sharing her food, drink, and toys with him even calling him her "best friend" embracing him in a hug before leaving with her mother. After yet more beatings from Brenda simply for eating her food, he'd return back to his room where he'd lament on his situation - namely how he missed his real family.

Just then, a gust of wind alerted Skye to his open window making him realize he could fit through, after thinking on things Skye packed his bags and lept out the window one goal in mind - find his real family. This would lead Skye to be involved in the Foliage Forestfire Incident, as he'd dash into the burning forest to rescue Andy.

Andrea "Andy" Naomi Adelle

Andy Adelle is a young fennec woman who's empathetic, kind, and caring she can see many sides of a situation. She has a strong sense of purpose believing she's alive to assist others she has a strong desire to give back to her community and improve the lives of people around her. She tends to not be very judgemental and seeks to understand that person first, and often shares her empathetic side in these situations.

She has little tolerance for people who don't seem to care how their actions affect others and understand what led them to act that way. However she may overlook her own needs in favor of others, often be self-critical when she feels like she's failed someone and may come off as cold and aloof when stressed not because she's mad at anyone inparticularly but because she overextended herself.

What Platforms?

Current plans are for Windows and Linux, consoles were being considered however I've decided to hild back on that as I wish to maintain my legal rights to the Skye's Origin IP. I'm debating on the license I wish to have for the game and am seeking to pursue a license that keeps the IP open to the public. (I don't mind if you all make fangames or fan-made content).

Yet, I don't want to be forced to sell the ownership rights to a Triple A Studio which is something you often have to do to get your game on consoles. As such while I initially had plans for Nintendo Switch, and a few other consoles I'll likely hold off on it until I figure something out.

Will the game be free? Will it have DRM?

Free? No. You will have to pay for the game, DRM? Also No. Once you buy it you'll be able to play with or without the client of your chosen storefront. I'd even like to include an installer with the game for the PC versions, regardless.
