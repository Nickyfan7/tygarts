Core of the Multiverse

Description

Universe where the Sonic the hedgehog video games take place, universe from which all else is spawned. In this universe Sonic, a young hedgehog born on Christmas Island left to seek out new adventures, this would lead him to take on various evils such as Dr. Ivo "Eggman" Robotnik, Chaos, Dark Gaia, Infinite, Starline, Surge, and many others with his journeys at times unveiling uncomfortable truths such as the tragedy bvehind Project Shadow, the downfall of the Echidna Tribe, the Gizoid, and the dangers of The End.
