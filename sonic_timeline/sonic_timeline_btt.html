(Past - 100 Years Before SegaSonic the Hedgehog)

Before Sonic

  • Dr. Ucano discovers he can liquify and amplify the power of magnets! He then proceeds to use the South Lakes Magnets, and Force Fortress and builds the Sky Empire.
    • Dr. Ucano also create 3 cores to automate the process - The Heater, The Cooler, and The Amplifier which Melts, Cools, and Amplifies the magnets respectively.
  • Civilians and Shopkeepers move in and establish heirarchy-based government. A listing of which would later be forged.
    • Emperor - The Sole Ruler of the Sky Empire, Figurehead.
    • Princess - Next in-line should the Emporer Step-down or Pass Away.
      • Note: Princess cannot assume rule if she is not of-age.
    • Council - High-ranking officials of the Sky Empire, usually wearing robes adorned with the Empire's Insignia, they made Important Decisions for the Empire.
    • Director of Sciences - The one who supplies the Sky Empire with it's technology - in this case that's Dr. Ucano.
  • As the Civilians settle in, and the government is established a golden era soon reigns over The Sky Empire and South Lakes.

Dr. Eggman's Arrival

  • Dr. Eggman arrives 100 years in the past, with nothing left to his name - No EGGATS-2013, No Chaos Emeralds, and No way back to his own time. Dr. Eggman uses some scraps he finds to make a cloaking device and explosives taking note of Dr. Ucano's schedule..
  • Dr.Eggman blows up the Emperor's Tower, amidst the panic he silently deports Dr. Ucano with a bomb before stealing his identity. Questioned about his change in appearance he states his face was under "severe hematoma".
  • Dr. Ucano disarms the bomb and begins work on an artificial life form known as "Polem". The Polem build their own undergound Monarchal Society which soon begins delcining in population as Polem leave and are never seen again.
    • Queen Polem - Sole Monarch of the Polem Kingdom.
    • Medela - Leading Polem Scientist, assigned by Queen Polem to asess the dwindilng population. She finds it's due to Polems giving into their killer intinct to eat amimals - begins work on cure to suppress instinct.
  • Dr. Eggman forges an old heirarchy listing, seeing as the Princess was not of-age and the Empreror passed away. The Council is reluctant with a member stating that he should not make public appearances, presumably being executed/deported.

  • Sonic journeys across South Island, collects all the Chaos Emeralds battling Dr. Eggman's 6 Master Robots. Soon arriving at the Scrambled Egg where after a battle with Mecha Sonic the gateway to Crystal Egg is opened.
  • Sonic enters and defeats Dr. Eggman once more - rescuing Tails as they both run off in Green Hill till nightfall.
